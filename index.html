About Us

Welcome to Dance Arts Centre, where passion meets precision.

For over 15 years, we've been the beating heart of the local dance scene, nurturing talent and igniting dreams. Nestled in the heart of Lichfield, our studio is a sanctuary for those who seek the graceful artistry of Ballet and the exhilarating athleticism of Acrodance. Led by a team of dedicated instructors, our academy prides itself on fostering a supportive environment where dancers of all ages and abilities can thrive. From tiny tots taking their first pli√© to seasoned performers perfecting their pirouettes, we believe in the power of dance to inspire, empower, and unite. At Dance Arts Centre, we don't just teach steps; we cultivate confidence, discipline, and creativity in every dancer who graces our studio. Whether you dream of dancing professionally or simply want to find joy in movement, we're here to help you reach your goals. Now, as we open our doors to new enrollments, we invite you to join our dance family. Book your spot today and let's dance into a brighter tomorrow together!

Miss Veronica (BA, ARAD, FISTD) is the principal of Dance Arts Centre. Veronica attended London Studio Centre, before travelling the world performing on cruise ships. She has been teaching for over 20 years, 15 of them here at Dance Arts Centre.

Miss Paulina attended Rambert, graduation with her BA in Dance Pedagogy. Paulina has danced with Nederlands Dance Theater for 3 years, before working as a choreographer for PO cruise ships, where she met Miss Veronica! She specialises in ballet technique here at Dance Arts Center.

Mr Arun is our head of Acrodance and body conditioning here at Dance Arts Centre. Mr Arun graduated from Bird College with a BA in Dance Education. Hs passion lies in strength and safe dance practise. He won gold at the UK Acrodance championship in 2019, 2021 and 2022.
