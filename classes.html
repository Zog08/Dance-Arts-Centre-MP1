Class Types

Classes here at Dance Arts Centre are taught by our fully qualified and experienced staff. To find out more about each genre we teach at the studios, please check out the information below:

In a ballet class, students learn a variety of techniques, movements, and skills that are fundamental to classical ballet. Throughout these lessons, emphasis is placed on proper technique, posture, alignment, and discipline. Ballet classes provide a holistic approach to dance education, fostering physical strength, flexibility, coordination, musicality, and artistic expression.

In an AcroDance class, students learn a fusion of acrobatics and dance techniques, creating a dynamic and expressive form of movement. Overall, acrodance classes provide a dynamic and challenging environment where students can develop both their acrobatic and dance skills while fostering creativity, athleticism, and artistic expression.

In our preschool ballet class, young children are introduced to the magical world of ballet through playful and imaginative activities. Preschool ballet classes provide a fun and nurturing introduction to ballet, fostering a love for dance and movement while promoting physical, cognitive, and social development in young children. We often use fun props such as ribbon, scraves, parachutes and floor spots too!

In our popular adult ballet class, participants learn classical ballet techniques tailored to the needs and abilities of adult learners whatever your existing skill level. These classes offer a fulfilling and rewarding experience for participants of all ages and backgrounds, providing a blend of physical activity, artistic expression, and personal growth in a supportive and encouraging atmosphere.
